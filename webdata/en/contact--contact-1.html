How to contact us

  • The easiest and fastest way to contact the wxCL project group is an eMail to the wxCL-devel mailing list.
  • The messages on the mailing list can also be browsed with a newsreader via NNTP (RFC 977). The address is nntp://news.gmane.org/gmane.lisp.wxcl.devel.
  • There are currently no distinct mailing lists for users and developers of wxCL.
  • Activities in the CVS tree are announced on the wxCL-cvs mailing list.
