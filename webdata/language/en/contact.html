The easiest and fastest way to contact the wxCL project group is an eMail to the wxCL-devel mailing list. There are currently no distinct mailing lists for users and developers of wxCL.

The messages on the mailing list can also be browsed with a newsreader via NNTP (RFC 977). The address is nntp://news.gmane.org/gmane.lisp.wxcl.devel.

Activities in the Subversion tree are announced on the wxCL-cvs mailing list.
